Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer any warranties on your work?

Yes, we offer a warranty on all labor and parts provided by our company. Specific warranty terms depend on the service and parts used but typically range from 1 to 5 years.

What qualifies as an electrical emergency?

Electrical emergencies include issues like exposed wires, smoke from an electrical device, loss of power, electrical fire, or any situation where there’s a risk of electrical shock. In such cases, call us immediately or dial 911 if it involves fire or life-threatening conditions.

Do you install smart home devices?

Yes, we install a variety of smart home devices including thermostats, security systems, and lighting solutions that can be controlled via smartphone apps or voice commands.
