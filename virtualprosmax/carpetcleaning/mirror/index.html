Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

For optimal results and to maintain a healthy home environment, it’s recommended to have your carpets cleaned every 6 to 12 months. However, high-traffic areas may need more frequent cleaning.

Are the cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Yes, we use eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for pets and children. Our methods are designed to be effective while ensuring a safe environment for your loved ones.

How long will it take for my carpets to dry?

Typically, carpets will take about 6 to 12 hours to dry completely. Factors such as humidity and air circulation can influence drying time, but we ensure to minimize any inconvenience.

What should I do to prepare for a carpet cleaning appointment?

Please remove any small items and furniture from the area to be cleaned. For larger furniture, our team can assist with moving it as needed. We also recommend vacuuming before our arrival for the best results.
