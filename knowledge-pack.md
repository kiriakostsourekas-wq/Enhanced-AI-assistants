# Business Knowledge Pack

## Business Summary

Northline AI helps appointment-based and enquiry-driven businesses turn more website enquiries into booked appointments.

The offer combines a website assistant, better lead capture, clearer next steps, and website improvements when needed.

The goal is practical: fewer stale enquiries, less repetitive back-and-forth, and a cleaner path from interest to booking.

## Positioning: What We Are / What We Are Not

What we are:
- A conversion-focused website and assistant service for businesses that depend on enquiries and appointments
- Best suited to businesses where speed, clarity, and follow-up quality affect booking rates
- Able to improve both the assistant and the surrounding website flow

What we are not:
- Not just a generic chatbot widget sold in isolation
- Not positioned as replacing staff
- Not sold on vague AI hype or unsupported promises
- Not presented as a full CRM, analytics, or integration stack unless discussed separately in a demo

## Who We Help

- Clinics and dental practices
- Med spas and beauty businesses
- Salons and aesthetic services
- Consultants and discovery-call businesses
- Home service businesses
- Other SMBs that rely on inbound enquiries, consultations, calls, or appointments

## Common Pain Points

- New enquiries sit too long before anyone replies
- Staff are too busy to answer consistently during the day or after hours
- People ask questions but never take the next booking step
- The website gets traffic but too few visitors become appointments
- Teams waste time answering the same early questions before a real booking conversation starts
- Leads come in with too little context, so follow-up is slower than it should be

## Diagnostic Angles

Useful bottlenecks to diagnose:
- slow follow-up after the enquiry comes in
- too many repetitive pre-booking questions for staff
- website visitors asking questions but not booking
- traffic coming in but weak conversion into appointments
- weak handoff from website enquiry to staff follow-up
- no clear next step after the first question

Useful diagnostic questions:
- Is the bigger issue slow follow-up, repetitive enquiries, or people dropping off before booking?
- Do leads come in but fail to turn into appointments?
- Is the bottleneck more on the website side or on the follow-up side?
- Are staff spending too much time answering the same early questions?

## What We Help With

- Answering common pre-booking questions
- Handling first response on the website
- Capturing useful lead details
- Guiding visitors to the right next step
- Improving the website so it supports conversion better
- Structuring a cleaner lead-to-booking flow

## Industry Fit Notes

### Dental Clinics

- Strong fit for new-patient enquiries, cosmetic treatment interest, whitening, implant consultations, emergency requests, and appointment availability questions
- A common issue is that front desk teams are busy, so website leads wait too long and cosmetic enquiries go cold
- The assistant can help answer common pre-booking questions and move the right enquiries toward a consultation request or next step

### Clinics

- Useful where patients ask about services, availability, private consults, insurance, or how to get started
- A common problem is that the site feels passive, so interested visitors leave before booking or submitting a useful enquiry

### Med Spas

- Good fit for treatment questions, consultation requests, downtime questions, and first-visit qualification
- Speed matters because people often compare providers quickly and move on

### Salons and Beauty Businesses

- Good fit when a lot of enquiries happen on mobile and staff end up handling too many repetitive DMs or booking messages
- Useful for higher-touch services where a consultation should happen before the booking

### Consultants

- Useful for discovery-call businesses that need better qualification before calendar time is spent
- Helps reduce low-fit calls by making the first interaction more structured

### Home Service Businesses

- Useful when speed wins the job, especially for urgent service requests and estimate enquiries
- Helps collect the details needed for faster follow-up or dispatch

## Website Scope

Northline can help both businesses that already have a website and businesses that need one built or improved.

The website is part of the conversion system, not a separate afterthought.

## Implementation Notes

- Setup should be described at a high level unless more specifics are confirmed in a demo
- Exact implementation details depend on the business, website, and workflow
- It is fine to explain the general process, but do not invent technical integrations, timelines, or unsupported features
- If someone wants technical specifics, the honest answer is that those details should be scoped in a demo

## Objection Notes

- A contact form still leaves delay, vague intake, and manual back-and-forth
- The assistant does not have to replace staff; it can handle first response and routine pre-booking questions before a human steps in
- If someone is cautious about AI, frame it as guided front-end support and structured first response, not hands-off automation or a human replacement

## What Happens After Booking a Demo

After a demo is booked, the next step is a discovery conversation.

That usually includes:
- Understanding the business and services
- Looking at how enquiries are handled today
- Identifying where leads stall or get lost
- Discussing what a better website, assistant, and booking flow could look like
- Recommending the most sensible next step if there is a fit

## FAQ Short Answers

**What exactly do you do?**
We help service businesses handle website enquiries better and turn more of them into booked appointments.

**Is this just a chatbot?**
No. The assistant is one part of the solution. The bigger goal is a better lead-to-booking flow.

**Can you improve our website too?**
Yes. We can improve or build the website if that is part of the conversion problem.

**Do you only work with businesses that already have a site?**
No. We can help if the site needs improvement or if one needs to be built.

**Who is this best for?**
It is strongest for appointment-based or enquiry-driven businesses where response speed and booking flow matter.

**What happens after I book a demo?**
We review your current enquiry flow, look at where leads are getting lost, and show what a better setup could look like for your business.

**We already have a contact form. Why would we need this?**
A form still leaves delay, vague intake, and manual back-and-forth. Northline adds structured first response, clearer next steps, and a better path from enquiry to booking.

**I don’t want AI talking to customers.**
That is a valid concern. Northline is not positioned as replacing staff; it supports early website enquiries and routine pre-booking questions so human follow-up can happen with better context.
