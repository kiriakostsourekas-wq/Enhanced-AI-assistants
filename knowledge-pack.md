# Business Knowledge Pack

## Business Summary

We help appointment-based small and medium businesses turn more enquiries into booked appointments.

Our service combines AI booking assistants, lead capture systems, and modern websites to help businesses respond faster, qualify leads better, and reduce missed opportunities. The goal is simple: fewer lost enquiries, less manual back-and-forth, and more booked appointments.

We are built for businesses that rely on inbound interest and need a better way to handle enquiries when staff are busy, unavailable, or inconsistent in follow-up.

---

## Who We Help

We help small and medium businesses, especially service businesses that depend on appointments, consultations, or qualified enquiries.

This includes businesses that:
- Receive leads through their website, ads, or social media
- Lose opportunities because replies are slow
- Struggle with manual follow-up
- Want a more modern booking experience
- Want their website to convert better

## Common FAQs

**What exactly do you do?**
We help businesses respond to enquiries faster and guide more leads toward booked appointments using AI assistants, conversion-focused websites, and better booking flows.

**Who is this for?**
This is best suited for appointment-based businesses such as clinics, dentists, med spas, salons, consultants, and service businesses that rely on inbound enquiries.

**Is this just a chatbot?**
No. The chatbot is only one part of the solution. The bigger goal is improving lead capture, qualification, and booking conversion.

**Can you also build or improve our website?**
Yes. We can create a new website or improve an existing one so it better supports lead conversion and booking.

**Do you work only with businesses that already have a website?**
No. We can help both businesses that already have a site and those that need one built.

**What problem does this solve?**
It helps reduce slow responses, missed leads, weak follow-up, and poor conversion from inbound interest to actual appointments.

**Do you target only one industry?**
No, but we focus mainly on appointment-based service businesses where response speed and booking conversion matter.

**What happens after someone books a demo?**
After booking, we review the business, understand the current lead flow, and discuss where opportunities are being lost. We then show how our solution could improve lead response, qualification, and booking outcomes.
